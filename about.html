About Us

FAQ

I am not an undergraduate student at UNC, can I still join?

Yes! Anyone interested in volleyball is allowed to join and participate in any of our events.

When can I join?

Our cut-off date for joining is after each semesterly break (aka Fall Break or Spring Break).

How can I join?

Please follow this link and fill out the required information to join us!

How much are dues and where do they go?

Club dues are $75/semester for new members and $30 for returning members. Dues help pay for equipment, travel, socials, and more!

Am I expected to show up to every practice?

Not at all! Please come to practice whenever you are able to, although we encourage coming as much as possible.

Where can I learn more?

We are active on Instagram, Facebook, and Twitter. You can also reach out to any of the staff members.

Our Mission

UNC Volleyball Club is committed to promoting teamwork, perseverance, and love for the game. Whether you're new to volleyball or have years of experience, our club offers a welcoming and competitive environment where all players can thrive.

Our Values & Notable Highlights

Our Values

  • Teamwork on and off the court
  • Respect for teammates, opponents, and the game
  • Dedication to personal growth and team success
  • Creating a positive and inclusive community

Notable Highlights

  • Regional Tournament Finalists – 2023
  • Fall Classic Tournament Champions – 2022
  • Raised over $2,000 for local charities
  • Over 75 active members in 2024

Our Journey

Founded in 1975 by a group of passionate students, UNC Volleyball Club has grown into one of the largest and most spirited sports clubs on campus. From humble beginnings in the old Woollen Gym, we now compete across the state and region with pride.

What to Expect

We welcome players of all skill levels and commitment levels. Whether you want to compete at the highest level or just enjoy pickup games with friends, there's a place for you. Expect intense rallies, lots of laughs, and teammates who become lifelong friends.
